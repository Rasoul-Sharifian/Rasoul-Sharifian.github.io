## About

I am an industrial Ph.D. student in computer vision, supervised by Professor Adrien Bartoli and Navid Rabbani. My research focuses on scene reconstruction, deformable registration, and augmented reality for minimally invasive surgery.

My Ph.D. is supported by a CIFRE contract, bringing industry and academia together to address real-world research challenges. Previously, I worked as a research engineer at EnCoV, Institut Pascal (CNRS). I also have experience with X-ray inspection systems and radiotherapy treatment planning—and several years of experience as a table tennis coach and player.

## Research Interests

- **Computer vision:** deformable registration, augmented reality, and computer-assisted intervention
